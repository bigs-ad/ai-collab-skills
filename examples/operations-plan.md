# Example: Operations Plan

## Prompt

Use `$manage-project`: "We have design, procurement, and launch preparation moving in parallel. Sync progress and tell me what should start now."

## Expected Route

1. `manage-project` separates active, blocked, ready, and not-yet-started work.
2. `delegate-work` prepares briefs for independent workstreams.
3. `check-work` validates gates before irreversible spending or launch decisions.

## Notes

GateReview is required for decisions that affect budget, compliance, public release, or irreversible external commitments.
